📋 How to Train Your Own Flood Detection Model

1
Go to teachablemachine.withgoogle.com and choose Image Project → Standard image model.
2
Create classes such as Flood / Rising Water, No Flood / Dry, and optionally Uncertain / Unclear. Upload or record sample images for each class.
3
Click Train Model. Once training completes, click Export Model → Upload (shareable link).
4
Copy the model URL (looks like https://teachablemachine.withgoogle.com/models/XXXXXXXX/) and paste it in the field above, then click Connect.
5
Click ▶ Start camera. The model will classify each frame in real time and trigger an alert if flood conditions are detected above 65% confidence.
