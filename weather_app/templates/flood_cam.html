📋 How the AI Flood Detection Works

1
When the page loads, a MobileNet v2 feature extractor is downloaded (~7 MB) and a classification head (Flood / Dry / Uncertain) runs on top — all in your browser via TensorFlow.js.
2
The model analyzes color distributions (brown/muddy tones, blue-grey tones), texture patterns (water surfaces, ripples), saturation & brightness, and edge features (horizontal water lines).
3
Click ▶ Start camera. Every frame is analyzed ~15–30 times per second. The AI outputs confidence percentages for each class.
4
If Flood confidence ≥ 65%, a red alert appears. Yellow caution at ≥ 45%. Green clear otherwise. No video data ever leaves your device — all inference is local.
