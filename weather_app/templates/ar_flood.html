ℹ️ How It Works

🗺️ Terrain Mesh

A 9×9 micro-elevation grid is fetched from Open-Meteo's elevation API, creating a 3D terrain mesh that reflects actual topography around your chosen location.

💧 Water Physics

The animated water plane rises based on your rainfall and duration settings. Water level is calculated using a simplified infiltration model based on terrain slope, elevation, and water proximity.

📷 Camera Overlay

Toggle your device camera to overlay the 3D simulation on a live feed, creating an augmented reality experience. Works best on mobile devices.

⚠️ Disclaimer

This is an educational visualization. Water behavior is simplified and does not account for drainage systems, soil type, or real-time river flow. Always follow official flood warnings.
